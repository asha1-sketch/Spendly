Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and password when you create an account, and any expense data you choose to enter, such as transaction amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your expense data to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We may also use your email address to send you important account notifications and service updates.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. You retain ownership of all data you enter into Spendly.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our service (such as hosting providers), but these providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export all your expense data from your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
